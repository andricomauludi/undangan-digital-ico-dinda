Our Story

How We Met

Our story began in the simplest way — through a mutual friend who thought we might get along. At the time, neither of us expected that this introduction would lead us to where we are today.

Not long after, we discovered that our offices were actually located quite close to one another. What started as occasional conversations slowly grew into something more meaningful.

We also found many things in common. Andrico spent his university years in Surabaya, while Dinda grew up there. That shared connection to the city created a familiarity between us.

In Jakarta, one of our favorite little traditions quickly formed — whenever we had the chance, we would meet up to explore unique and affordable culinary spots around the city. Those simple food adventures turned into countless conversations, laughter, and moments that slowly built the foundation of our relationship.

From a simple introduction to a journey filled with shared stories, we are grateful for every step that led us here.

Engagement Story

Our Intimate Moment

On February 15th, 2026, we celebrated a meaningful milestone in our journey together — our engagement ceremony held at Ellenoir, Surabaya.

The day was intentionally kept intimate, attended only by our closest family members from both sides. It was never meant to be a grand celebration, but rather a moment for the people who mean the most to us.

The event itself was beautifully simple. A shared dining table where families gathered, conversations flowed warmly, and a small backdrop where we spoke from the heart in front of our loved ones.

Though the setting was modest, the atmosphere was filled with warmth, joy, and genuine happiness. It was a reminder that the most meaningful moments are often the simplest ones — surrounded by family, love, and sincerity.
